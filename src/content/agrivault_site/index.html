The platform

One stack. Every crop. Any market.

Built for field reality — works without internet, syncs when connectivity is available.

Primary Farmer Registry

Permanent unique IDs. GPS-mapped plots. The identity infrastructure that makes farmers visible to capital and markets.

 Primary Production Dashboard

County-level production vs targets. Import gap tracker. Post-harvest loss alerts. The Minister's live view of food security.

 Compliance Audit Trail

Every action logged. Chain-of-custody from farm gate to export port. Accepted by any buyer in any market.
